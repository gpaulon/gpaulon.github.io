Biography


I am Giorgio Paulon, a fifth-year PhD candidate in Statistics and Data Sciences at the University of Texas at Austin. I am working under the joint supervision of Abhra Sarkar and Peter Müller. I am generally interested in Bayesian nonparametric models that solve applied problems.

I am originally from Udine, Italy, and I obtained both my BSc and MSc at Politecnico Di Milano in Mathematical Engineering. I also spent two years at École Centrale Paris, France, for a double degree program (T.I.M.E).

I love photography and I am passionate about travelling. Photography is a mental exercise that helps me to focus and to relax. Travelling, especially in uncomfortable and far away places, gives me many motivations and helps me finding the unexpected outside of my daily routine.
